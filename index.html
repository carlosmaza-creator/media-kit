About

Carlos Maza is a media critic and video essayist whose work on free speech, media manipulation, and fascist propaganda has reached over 20 million viewers on YouTube.

His Vox series Strikethrough covered media and politics during the Trump era. His investigation into YouTube hate speech directly contributed to the platform overhauling its content moderation policies.

He is currently completing Free Speech Bad: How Our Free Speech Fetish Is Breaking Democracy, arguing that the liberal obsession with free speech is aiding the rise of fascism.
